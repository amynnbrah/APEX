Our Services

Exterior Painting

Applying paint to the outside of a building to protect it from the elements and enhance its appearance.

Interior Painting

Applying paint to the inside of a building to enhance its aesthetic appeal and create a welcoming atmosphere.

Staining

Applying a transparent or semi-transparent finish to wood surfaces to enhance their natural beauty and protect them from damage.

Exposed Aggregate

Cleaning the surface of exposed aggregate concrete to remove dirt, grime, and other substances that can dull its appearance, revealing the decorative aggregate beneath.

About Us

Welcome to APEX Painting, where family values and craftsmanship intersect to create exceptional results. With over two decades of dedicated service, we take pride in being a family-owned and operated business, deeply rooted in our local community.

Our Heritage: Founded over 20 years ago by Travis W Anardi, our company has flourished under the guiding principles of integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction. What started as a humble endeavor has evolved into a trusted name in Idaho Painting.

Family Values: At APEX Painting, we believe in more than just providing services; we believe in building relationships. As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of trust and reliability. Every project we undertake is infused with the same care and attention to detail that we would apply to our own homes.

Craftsmanship and Experience: With decades of experience under our belt, our team brings unparalleled expertise to every job. Whether it's outdoor painting, staining, indoor painting, remodeling, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Community Commitment: As a local business, we are deeply committed to giving back to the community that has supported us over the years. From sponsoring local events to volunteering our time and resources, we strive to make a positive impact beyond our business endeavors.

Our Promise: When you choose APEX Painting, you're not just hiring a service provider; you're joining our extended family. We promise to treat your project with the same dedication and care that we would apply to our own. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we won't rest until we've achieved excellence.

Thank you for considering APEX Painting for your aesthetic needs. We look forward to the opportunity to serve you and welcome you into our family.
